
Example One.

For sweet tooths.

Ingredients.
2 lbs chocolate chips.
100 oz sugar.
3 oz lard.
42 t salt.

Cooking time: 5 minutes.

Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees.

Method.

Put chocolate chips into mixing bowl.
Add sugar.
Combine lard.
Put salt into mixing bowl.
Pour contents of mixing bowl into baking dish.

Serves 1.
